Hammer curl

Exercise of the day

Hammer curl is a fantastic exercise for biceps,
especially for the outter part of the bicep muscle.
On the photo you can see how to do this exercise correctly!
The exercise is done with a grip with wrists turned to
90 degrees clockwise. The performance should be slow and correct!
